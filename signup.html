Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Overview

Liverary is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our digital library platform. Because Liverary is a demonstration system, all data handling is local to your device.

2. Information We Collect

When you register for a Liverary account, we collect:

  • Account Information: Your first and last name, and email address

  • Preferences: Any settings or customizations you configure

3. How We Store Your Data

This is a demo application. All data you provide is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device. No information is transmitted to, or stored on, any external server or database. Your data never leaves your browser.

4. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally is used solely to:

Since all data is stored locally on your device, Liverary does not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. We have no access to the data stored in your browser's localStorage.

Your data persists in localStorage until you explicitly clear it. You can delete all your Liverary data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or localStorage. Upon deletion, all account information, reading history, and preferences will be permanently removed.

Liverary does not use tracking cookies. We only use browser localStorage for session management and data persistence. No third-party analytics or advertising cookies are used on this platform.
